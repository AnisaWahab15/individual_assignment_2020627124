My UiTM Experience




Association Of Information Scientists (AIS)

Association Of Information Scientists (AIS) is a club under Faculty Of Information Management for diploma students and involvement of club members are only for information management student from semester 1 until semester 5. As for me, I was involve with this club by becoming club members as secretariat for AIS since semester 1 and I was appointed as Multimedia and Innovation EXCO in semester 3 until semester 5. While hold the position offered, there many new experiences I gain and also there are many new things I learn like design a poster, making montage video, writing official letter for program and also involving many program like international events, annually faculty events and also private special events. However, while holding and serve for this club, we as club members also had to face many kind of challenges, as for in previous Faculty Academic Award of 2022, I was in charge of the food. Due to our bad luck, all food that had being serve was stale and all the student were not given food for the event. No matter what happen all the events was run smoothly and everything that happened, every Cloud Has a Silver Lining for us as organizer.
